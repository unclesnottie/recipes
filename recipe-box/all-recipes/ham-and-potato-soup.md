Ham and Potato Soup
===================

Ingredients
-----------

Amount     | Ingredient
---------- | ----------------------------------
8 cups     | Russet potatoes, diced
1 ct.      | Yellow onion, diced
2 ct.      | Carrots, large, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup    | Celery, chopped
16 oz.     | Ham, cubed
1 tsp.     | Kosher salt
1/4 tsp.   | Black pepper
1/4 cup    | Flour
4 cups     | Chicken broth or stock
1 1/2 cups | Heavy Cream
1/2 cup    | Sour cream

Directions
----------

1.  add the potatoes, onion, carrot, celery, ham, kosher salt, pepper and chicken broth to your slow cooker.  Cook on low 7-8 hours or high 4-5 hours.
2.  Using a potato masher, mash about 1/3 of the potatoes.  Don't worry about the ham, it won't really mash much.
3.  Add the flour, heavy cream and sour cream.  Stir everything together.

Tips
----

1.  We add Salamida Pinch Cajun Spice Blend to it for extra yummy flavor.
